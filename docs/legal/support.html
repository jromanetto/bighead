Frequently Asked Questions

How do I restore my purchases?

Go to Profile > Settings > Restore Purchases. Your premium subscription will be restored automatically.

How do I cancel my subscription?

Subscriptions are managed through the App Store. Go to Settings > Apple ID > Subscriptions on your iPhone to manage or cancel.

I found an incorrect question. How can I report it?

Send us an email at support@bighead-app.com with the question details and we'll fix it as soon as possible.

How do I delete my account?

Send a request to privacy@bighead-app.com and we will delete your account and all associated data within 30 days.

The app is not working properly. What should I do?

Try closing and reopening the app, or reinstalling it. If the problem persists, contact us with details about the issue.

Response Time
