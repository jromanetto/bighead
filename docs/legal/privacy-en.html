Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 28, 2026

Introduction

BIGHEAD ("we", "our", "the app") is committed to protecting your privacy. This privacy policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your personal information when you use our quiz application.

Data We Collect

We collect the following data:

  • Account identifiers: email address (if you create an account)
  • Game data: scores, statistics, achievements, progress
  • Technical data: device type, operating system, anonymous identifier
  • Notification token: to send push notifications (if enabled)

You can use the app anonymously without providing any personal information.

How We Use Your Data

Your data is used to:

  • Save your progress and scores
  • Display leaderboards and compare your performance
  • Send daily reminder notifications (if enabled)
  • Improve user experience and fix bugs
  • Manage premium subscriptions via RevenueCat

Data Sharing

We never sell your personal data. Your data may be shared with:

  • Supabase: database hosting (servers in Europe)
  • RevenueCat: in-app subscription management
  • Expo: push notification service

Data Retention

Your data is retained as long as your account is active. Anonymous account data may be deleted after 12 months of inactivity.

You can request deletion of your data at any time by contacting us.

Your Rights (GDPR)

Under GDPR, you have the following rights:

  • Right of access: obtain a copy of your data
  • Right to rectification: correct your information
  • Right to erasure: delete your account and data
  • Right to data portability: receive your data in a standard format
  • Right to object: refuse certain processing

To exercise these rights, contact us at: privacy@bighead-app.com

Security

We implement technical and organizational security measures to protect your data:

  • Encryption of data in transit (HTTPS/TLS)
  • Secure authentication
  • Restricted access to personal data
  • Hosting on secure servers in Europe

Children

BIGHEAD is intended for a general audience. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13 without parental consent.

Changes

We may update this privacy policy. Significant changes will be notified in the app. We encourage you to review this page regularly.

Contact

For any questions about this privacy policy:

Email: privacy@bighead-app.com
